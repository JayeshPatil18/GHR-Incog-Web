GHR Incog: Fearless Voices, Anonymous Choices

GHR Incog is a mobile application designed to provide a secure and anonymous platform for college students, specifically tailored for G. H. Raisoni College of Engineering and Management, Pune. The app aims to empower users to freely share their thoughts, messages, secrets, and confessions with their college peers without revealing their identities.


Key Features:

1. Anonymous User Interaction:

Users are assigned unique anonymous usernames upon registration, ensuring complete privacy and confidentiality. This allows them to engage in open and honest communication without fear of judgment or repercussion.

2. College Community Focus:

GHR Incog is exclusively designed for the college community, with access restricted to students using their college email IDs. This fosters a sense of trust and familiarity, creating a safe space for students to express themselves within the confines of their academic environment.

3. Thoughtful Expression:

The app provides a platform for users to share their thoughts, feelings, and confessions with the community, creating an environment of empathy and understanding. Users can engage in meaningful conversations and connect with others who may share similar experiences.

4. Message Circulation:

Messages are circulated within the college community, enabling students to stay informed about the thoughts and concerns of their peers. This creates a sense of unity and encourages open dialogue among students.

5. Secure and Moderated Environment:

GHR Incog ensures a secure environment by implementing robust security measures and content moderation. This guarantees a positive user experience and prevents any misuse or inappropriate content.



The development of GHR Incog is driven by the belief that fostering anonymous expression within the college community can contribute to building a supportive and empathetic atmosphere. By providing a platform for authentic communication, the app aims to strengthen connections among students and enhance the overall college experience.
